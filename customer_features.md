# Profitly - Smart Business Ledger
## Key Features & Benefits for Small Business Owners

Profitly is designed to simplify your daily business operations. Here are the key features that will help you manage your business more efficiently:

### 1. 📦 Smart Inventory Management
* **Never run out of stock:** Automatically track how many items you have left. The app highlights "Low Stock" items in red so you know exactly what to reorder.
* **Easy Bulk Restocking:** Just got a new delivery? Use the **Bulk Add** feature to quickly type in the new quantities and prices for all your products on a single screen—no need to open each product individually. You can even set a "Default Category" to apply to all new items at once.
* **Profit Previews:** When adding a new product, simply enter the Buy Price and Sell Price, and the app instantly shows you your profit margin per item.

### 2. 🛒 Buy Stock (Purchases)
* **Record Supplier Bills:** Easily record new purchases from suppliers. You can log the total bill amount, invoice number, and add notes.
* **Flexible Payments:** Pay in full, pay a partial amount, or take it entirely on Udhaar (credit). The app automatically calculates how much you still owe the supplier and updates their ledger balance.
* **Auto-Amount in Words:** To prevent typos, the app automatically translates large numbers (like ₹12,500) into words ("Twelve Thousand Five Hundred Rupees") as you type.

### 3. ➕ Quick POS (Point of Sale)
* **Fast Checkout:** Add items to a customer's cart quickly. The app calculates the total amount instantly.
* **Customer Udhaar:** Just like supplier bills, you can record a sale as Fully Paid, Partial Payment, or Udhaar. The pending amount is automatically added to the customer's ledger.
* **Real-time Inventory Sync:** Every time you make a sale, the app automatically deducts those items from your inventory.

### 4. 📒 Smart Khata (Ledger)
* **Everything in One Place:** View all your pending payments (Udhaar) from customers and your pending dues to suppliers on a single screen.
* **Color-Coded Clarity:** 
  * **Green (You Will Get):** Money that customers owe you.
  * **Red (You Will Give):** Money that you owe to suppliers.
* **Settle Balances:** Easily record payments received from customers or payments made to suppliers to clear their pending balances.

### 5. 📊 P&L and Analytics
* **Know Your Profits:** Instantly see your Today's Sales, Today's Profit, and your Profit Margin percentage.
* **Month-wise Breakdown:** Track your business performance over time with a calendar view showing sales and profit for each day.
* **Data Privacy (Eye Toggle):** If you are sitting in your shop with customers around, click the 👁️ icon on the Dashboard or P&L screen to hide your sensitive sales and profit numbers (shows as `₹ ****`).

### 6. 📱 Simple & Professional Design
* **Works Everywhere:** Designed to work smoothly on your mobile phone, tablet, or computer.
* **No Accounting Knowledge Needed:** The app uses simple terms (like "You Got", "You Gave") instead of complex accounting jargon (like "Debits" and "Credits").
* **Fast and Responsive:** Everything loads instantly without making you wait.
